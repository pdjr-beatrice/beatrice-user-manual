11. Hydraulics

The ship's hydraulic system is powered by a permanently engaged pump connected to the main engine gearbox power take-off port.

Pressurised oil is pumped to the adjacent reservoir and distribution unit. The distribution unit valve block includes an 'orbitrol' system for assisted steering and two electrically operated load-sense valves, one high-pressure, one low-pressure, which feed fluid to a subsidiary valve block located in the fore-locker which in turn operates the anchor windlass motor, mast hydraulic cylinder and bow-thruster motor.

Do not alter any of the system settings

Do ensure that the oil level in the reservoir remains topped up
