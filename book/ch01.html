This owner's manual describes the Dutch-built Luxe-motor live-aboard barge Beatrice (registered in the UK as Beatrice of Hull).

Beatrice was commissioned in 2015 by Paul and Diane Reeve <paulanddiane@pdjr.eu> from the builder Scheepsreparatie Friesland (SRF) based in Harlingen, NL. The ship was launched in Harlingen on 6 August 2017 with hand-over in August 2018.

This manual describes the ship and its installed equipment together with explanations of its systems and guidance on their operation: it is not a comprehensive or definitive text and documentation supplied by the manufacturers of all installed equipment is considered an essential and authoritative adjunct.

This manual and manufacturer's manuals are intended to be useful orientation and maintenance guides, but in case of difficulty you should refer to the boat builder or the manufacturer of the installation in question.

Always use trained and competent people for maintenance, repairs and modifications: in particular, modifications that may affect the safety characteristics of the craft must be assessed, executed and documented by competent people and the boat builder cannot be held responsible for modifications which they have not approved.

PLEASE KEEP THIS MANUAL IN A SECURE PLACE, AND HAND IT OVER TO THE NEW OWNER WHEN YOU SELL THE CRAFT
