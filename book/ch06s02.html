2. Small repairs

Small repairs to the hull can be made by using a good quality epoxy filler and paint. But take care: this only goes if you know what you are doing, and the damage is not structural. If in the damaged area there show small cracks in the surrounding area or if the damaged area shows fibres in the hull or deck structure, get an expert repairman to do the job and/or assess the damages. If structural damages occurs while underway, try to close off the repair area by taping it.

Remember that any repair must be as good as the original work if you want to keep your boat in good shape.

DO NOT use silicone-based sealants on any part of the boat.

When sanding, painting or doing similar work on the boat, remember that paintdust and epoxies are to be treated as toxic chemical waste.
