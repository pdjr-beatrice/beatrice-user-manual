1. Craft Identification Number

Figure 2.1. CIN plate


The Craft Identification Number (CIN) (formerly known as the Hull Identification Number (HIN)) is affixed on the upper starboard side of the transom of the boat.

The CIN includes data on the country of production, producer, type of boat, yard build number, year of construction and model year.

Do not alter, remove or damage the CIN.
